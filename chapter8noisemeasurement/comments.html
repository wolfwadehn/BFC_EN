Chapter 8 Noise Measurement
/
Comments

Comments

1. The noise of the water cooler during heat dissipation is about 10 decibels higher than the normal measured value.

2. The noise of stainless steel 16mm nitrogen cutting when encountering small size graphics and tooth plates will be 10dB-30dB higher than the normal measured value.

3. Stainless steel has the highest noise value when piercing and cutting is completed, which can reach about 110 decibels in an instant.
