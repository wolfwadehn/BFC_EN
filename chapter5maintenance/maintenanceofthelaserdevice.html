Maintenance of the laser device

Maintenance of the laser device

In general, fiber laser device is designed to be maintenance-free. No components, parts or mechanical items inside the fiber laser device are required to be replaced within its service life. However, it’s still recommended to inspect the fiber laser device on a regular basis.

1.1Regular inspection

Inspect the following to check if there is any damage that may affect the operational safety of the fiber laser device. Frequency of inspection is determined by the installation environment of the fiber laser device.

Housing
Piping
LLK connector
Electrical and gas connection
Water hose and accessory

1.2 General cleaning

Clean the housing occasionally to make sure that the warning signs are fully and clearly visible to the user. Before cleaning, please disconnect the power supply of the laser device and wipe the housing with clean wet cloth. Please contact Jiangsu Jinfangyuan CNC Machine Co., Ltd. Immediately for replacement parts if the warning sign is displaced or detached.

Do not operate the laser if any missing or damaged warning signs of hazards is not known and properly understood by you.

1.3 Cleaning the optical parts

Cleanliness of the surface is critical to all optical parts. Optical parts must be protected from dirt or dust in the environment or contamination caused by improper handling with greasy fingers. Otherwise, the surface of the optics will be eroded by the contamination, which will result in permanent damage to the equipment.

To avoid unnecessary damage, only properly trained and authorized personnel are allowed to clean the optics.
