Table 1-2

Terms Explanation

Machine

According to the definition from the EU Machinery Directive, a machine (complete machine) is the entirety of interconnected assemblies and components, of which at least one component is movable. A machine is designed for a specific application.

Automation

Complementary equipment and control system components to ensure a long-time and continuous production process without intervention of operator.

Automation components

Components used to move the object into or out of the machine automatically.

Laser

Laser (Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation) is a term from physics. It describes the physical effect, although in common parlance often the device, with which laser radiation is generated.

Laser device

A laser processing device consisting of a beam generator and a laser control as well

as connections for the power supply and a process cooler/chiller.

User

The user is the person who is responsible for the operation of the machine or system and has the authority to issue orders to employees.

Personal safety equipment(PPE)

Equipment intended to be used or worn by persons to protect themselves against hazards to their safety and health.
