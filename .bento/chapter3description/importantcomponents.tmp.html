1.1 Nameplate

The nameplate is located at the right bottom corner at the rear side of machine.

1.2 Machine frame

The machine frame is designed as a welded steel construction. Internal stress of the machine is eliminated by the vibration stress relief process. As a result of precise machining, the machine frame offers a horizontal and stable platform for the motion unit .

In addition, the machine is designed with part removal plates to guide the scraps the slags from cutting all the way into the slag cart underneath the machine body. Due to the intense heat and strong adhesion of the slags, the part removal plates need to be cleaned weekly.

On each side of the machine body, a high-precision linear ball bearing guide rail is installed along Y axis. This ensures the movement accuracy of the crossbeam on Y-axis direction. A high-precision rack is installed next to the guide rail on both sides. The rack engages with the pinion to move the crossbeam on Y-axis direction. The high-precision linear ball bearing guide rail and the rack are lubricated by central lubrication.

The linear guide rails are covered with flexible bellow-style protective covers. Use the protective covers correctly so that they can slide smoothly. Do not press the protective covers. Otherwise, the protective covers may get deformed or damaged. Please take proper care of the protective covers. This helps to keep dust away from the racks and guide rails, extend their service life and maintain the transmission accuracy of the machine.

In addition to the protection of system software limits and electrical switches, the motion unit of the crossbeam is clad with polyurethane for collision protection.

1.3 Crossbeam

Crossbeam is connected to the linear guide rails on the Y-axis direction. This closed gantry structure with dual drives features a high rigidity and lightweight construction, which makes it easy to achieve high speed and dynamics.

The crossbeam movement is driven by the servo motors and transmitted by the rack and pinion systems on two sides. This enables fast positioning. quicker response and thus better dynamics of the crossbeam. In addition, two high-precision linear ball bearing guide rails are mounted on the crossbeam and connected to the motion components of X axis. X-axis rack is installed on the crossbeam and engages with the pinion of the Z-axis servo components. High-precision linear ball bearing guide rail and rack are lubricated by central lubrication and covered by the flexible protective covers. Use the protective covers correctly so that they can slide smoothly. Do not press the protective covers. Otherwise, the protective covers may get deformed or damaged. Please take proper care of the protective covers. This helps to keep dust away from the high precision racks and guide rails, extend their service life and maintain the transmission accuracy of the machine.

In addition to the protection of system software limits and electrical switches, the servo unit of Z axis, the motion unit on X axis, two ends of the crossbeam are clad with polyurethane for collision protection.

1.4 Servo system

Most components of the servo system are made of aluminum alloy, which significantly reduces its weight. This is beneficial to the movement of servo system along the laser beam transmission direction, reducing the inertial force when starting or stopping the servo system and ensuring the transmission quality of the light path. In addition, the servo slide is connected to the guide carriages to ensure precise guiding. Movement of the servo components on X-axis direction is driven by the automatic digital servo motor and transmitted by the rack and pinion system to ensure precise movement. Z-axis movement is driven by automatic digital servo motor and transmitted by coupling and ball screw. Cutting head is mounted on the Z-axis servo slide.

1.5 Pallet changer

Pallet changer consists of two identical pallets with toothed slats. During production, one pallet is moved to the machining position inside the machine. The other pallet is moved to the loading and unloading position outside the machine. Positions of the two pallets can be exchanged. For optimized processing time, the pallet can be loaded and unloaded while machining. As a result, the downtime for loading and unloading in the past is saved now and the productivity is improved. In particular, loading and unloading of thicker or larger sheet require longer time, which now can be saved due to loading and unloading parallel to production.

The frame of the pallet changer is constructed by welding together special-shaped steel tubes, which gives the frame a high rigidity. The pallet is designed with a grid frame which is supported by sheet metals and used as a worktable for the workpieces to be processed. Toothed slats are mounted on the grid frame and can be removed and replaced conveniently if ablation occurs to the teeth. In addition, the toothed slats can be cut using the laser cutting machine. This makes maintenance of the machine easier. Use of new tooth slats also reduce abrasion and scratch on the sheet to be processed, lower the spatter and improve the cutting result.

During the pallet change process, the pallet is pulled by the chain and guided by the guide rail. In addition, the toothed slats installed on the pallet can be exchange easily and quickly.

1.6 Suction system

During laser cutting of the sheet, a certain amount of dust will be generated. If dust-extraction measures are not taken, the cutting result and the workshop environment will be affected. For this reason, the machine is equipped with a suction system mainly consisting of the blower and pipes. The main pipe of the suction system is installed inside the machine body and connect to the blower via pipe.

1.7 Slag removal system

During the laser cutting process, a large amount of scraps and slags will be generated. These scraps and slags will be guided by the part removal plates into the slag cart underneath the machine body. Each slag cart has four casters which make it easy to push, pull and drag around. The handle of the slag cart can be rotated upward or downward and disconnected from the slag cart. The slag cart is positioned below the part removal plates. When the scarps and slags pile up, the slag cart can be pulled out from side of the machine. After emptying, push the slag cart along the guide plate underneath the machine to the designated position until next emptying.

1.9 Gas supply system

Three assist gases are supplied via the gas circuit are: oxygen, nitrogen and compressed air. Cutting of different materials requires different gases.

When cutting mild steel sheet, open the oxygen valve. The depressurized oxygen is regulated to the set pressure by low-pressure proportional valve and output to the cutting head.

When cutting stainless steel, a higher cutting pressure is required. Open the nitrogen valve. The depressurized nitrogen is regulated to the set pressure by the high-pressure proportional valve and output to the cutting head.

Cutting quality of assist gases:

Mild steel cutting (Si content: 0.04%)

Oxygen: Purity 99.9% Oxidization on the cut edge.

Stainless steel cutting (high pressure cutting):

Nitrogen: Purity 99.8%, cut edge has no oxidization and is slightly yellowish.

Nitrogen: Purity 99.95%, cut edge has no oxidization and is not always bright.

Nitrogen: Purity 99.995%, cut edge has no oxidization and is always bright.

Some of the gases (e.g. gases from spraying paint or cleaning of machine with organic solvents) can be absorbed by the laser beam and cause adverse impact on the system operation. In addition, there is a risk of explosion when these gases come into contact with the laser beam

1.10 Lubrication system

Z-axis module is lubricated by a self-lubrication mechanism.

1.11 Light transmission system

Light path of the CNC laser cutting machine: The laser beam generated by the laser device is transmitted by laser light cable (LLK) to collimation lens and cutting head in sequence. The workpiece stays still and the cutting head moves. Motion load of the machine is constant. After commissioning, the machine dynamics will not change with varying sheet sizes.

1.12 Safety light curtain

Safety light curtain surrounding the KS06Q area is a protection system consisting of controller, sensor (including transmitter and receiver), transmission cable of the reflector, power cable and accessories.

The beam is emitted from the transmitter, deflected by the reflector and received by the receiver. The danger zone is fenced off by the beams travelling from the transmitter to the reflector and finally arriving the receiver.

Safety light curtain use light beams to detect interruptions. The minimum beam spacing is 40mm and can be widened by an increment of 40mm. The user may choose appropriate beam spacing according to the requirements of the on-site protection. An integrated collimation beam system is used to assist the beam alignment.

1.14 Manual pressure release valve

A manual pressure release valve is installed on the main gas supply line at the instrument panel. The operating personnel is required to release the residual pressure inside the gas cylinder and hoses before carrying out maintenance or service work on the gas cylinder. This helps to avoid unexpected movement of the gas cylinder and ensures the safety of the operating personnel. Turn the manual pressure release valve to EXH position and secure with a padlock. Do no unlock until the maintenance or service work is completed. After unlocking, turn the manual pressure release valve back to SUP position.
