7.1 Packaging and Rigging

Accessories delivered with the machine and auxiliary tools are packed in an iron box. The main body of BFC3015 is transported in a container. Except for BFC3015, main body of all other machine types are transported individually in a packing box in compliance with export requirements. Laser device is transported in wooden box in compliance with export requirements.

When handling the main machine body, lifting equipment is required. Secure the load at the 4 eyelet positions using a steel chain with a load capacity of above 10T.
When handling the laser device, lifting equipment is required. Secure the load at the 4 eyelet positions using a sling with a load capacity of above 1T.
After the laser device is packed, transport the laser device to the target position using a forklift truck with a load capacity of above 1T.
When handling the the process cooler, lifting equipment is required. Secure the load at the 4 eyelet positions using a sling with a load capacity of above 1T.

7.2 Transport

The arrangement and transport of the system from the truck to its final installation site need to be prepared and carried out by the customer. Before the machine is delivered, the customer must report the transport route of the machine to the installation site. In particular, the following must be checked:

Are gateway openings, header heights and cable rack heights sufficient?
Can the floor be crossed with armored rollers, etc.?

Transport equipment and tools：

Truck crane (80T)
Forklift truck (Load capacity : 2.5T)
Indoor crane (Load capacity: 15T)
Armored roller
Hydraulic jack
Crowbar and extensions

Measure to be taken by the user:

All transport-related work must be carried out in accordance with relevant transport regulations.
Foundation of the installation site must meet the requirements of the installation conditions.
Unload the machine from the truck using a crane truck with sufficient lifting capacity.
The laser device, process cooler and suction system can be transferred directly from truck to the machine’s installation site using a forklift truck .
Use armoured roller to transport the machine from outdoors to the workshop.
Use indoor crane or armoured roller with sufficient lifting capacity to transport the machine to the installation site.
Position hydraulic jack at the installation site in alignment with armoured roller.

Notes：

Exposed machined surface on the machine should be treated for rust prevention.
Avoid hazards from the environment in which machine is transported, e.g. rain, moisture, tilting, rodent infestation and pothole etc., and keep good ventilation.
Do not climb, stand or place heavy objects on the product package.
Do not drag or pull cables connected to the product.
Do no impact or scratch the control panel or the display.
Product package should avoid exposure to moisture, sunlight or rain.
When lifting the machine, raise and set down the machine carefully. Collision is strictly prohibited. Throughout the lifting process, the steel sling must not scratch or impact on the machine. In case of any faults, the machine must be isolated with soft object to avoid such problems.

7.3 Storage conditions, period and notes

The machine should be stored in an environment free from hazards such as rain, moisture, tilting, rodents and potholes. Keep the environment well ventilated. The storage environment temperature should range from 0℃~ +40℃ and the relative humidity should not exceed 80%. It’s strictly prohibited to store the machine in the open air. If temporary open-air storage is required for various reasons, in addition to meeting the above requirements, the storage location and packaging conditions should be checked at any time necessary to ensure that the machine is not damaged.

7.4 Unboxing and precautions

Unboxing precautions:

If the wooden box is used for transport, please follow the instructions on the outside to open the wooden box (by service technician from JFY) in order to avoid damage to the equipment inside.

If the equipment, e.g. the machine etc., is packaged with protective film, please do not cut open the protective film with a sharp-edge object in order to prevent scratch on equipment surface or damage to the pipes (consult service technician from JFY at the time of opening).

Checking delivery:

Check if the product is what you ordered after unpacking.
Check for transport damage of all equipment parts.
Check the packing list for any missing or damaged components.
Contact us promptly if there is any discrepancy in the products, missing accessory or transport damage.
