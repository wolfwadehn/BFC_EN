Organization

A professional gas installation is a basic prerequisite for smooth commissioning and trouble-free operation of the system. Therefore, pass on the "Gas supply" section to the commissioned specialist company for industrial gases in good time in accordance with the planning aid.

Urgent recommendation from JFY: The user should consult his gas supplier regarding the gas supply.
Installation must be performed by a qualified industrial gas company.
The appropriate standards must always be observed.

Short circuit or fire hazard when gas lines

and electrical cables are laid together!

Do not lay gas lines together with electrical lines in a single cable duct.
Keep gas lines separate up to the connection point on the machine.

General requirements

Gas transport system are to be constructed as follows:

Piping from the supply unit to the shop wall must be installed with a stop valve.
Use pipe or appropriate air hose from shop wall up to the connection point of the machine.
The evaporator must be dimensioned according to the maximum gas consumption of all connected machines. The line from the evaporator to the connection point must be kept as short as possible.
A stop valve must be installed at the tapping point in every individual supply line to the machine.
The gas installation must be designed in such a way that the supply lines to the connection point of the machine and the connection point itself are unable to ice up.
Gas temperature must not exceed 50 ℃ (120 °F ). This is of particular importance if the required gas pressure is generated by means of pressure booster system.

A manually operated on-off valve must be installed on the intake pipe of each machine to ensure that there is no residual gas in the pipe during maintenance.

If it is necessary to rotate the pressure relief screw in maintenance mode, unscrew it slowly. Stop screwing when you hear a pressure release sound. Due to the high air pressure, it is necessary to keep enough distance to prevent the pressure relief screw from popping out and hurting people. After the maintenance, it is required to tighten the loosened pressure relief screw again to ensure that there is no air leakage during use.

To avoid damage to machine parts due to the pressure impact, please open or close the manual valve slowly.

4.2 Cutting gas supply with cylinders or bundles

Cylinders or bundles

The easiest and most economical way of supplying cutting gas is with pressure reducers for cylinders or bundles. The transport requirements are higher however, due to the volume of consumption. One bundle consists of twelve cylinders.
The gas flow is interrupted when changing cylinder or bundle.

Pressure safety device with specified pressure regulators connected to the supply unit must be installed. Pressure regulators must be secured against the maximum input pressure (inherently safe).

4.3 Cutting gas supply with gas tank

A tank system is appropriate for safe gas supply at a gas consumption (volume under standard conditions) of about 200 - 400 m³ (0.7 cfm - 1.4 cfm) per week.
The optimum tank size depends on the tapping quantity and on the local conditions.
The customer should consult the gas supplier.

The requirements for high-pressure cutting with nitrogen are as follows:

Do not use a tapping point pressure regulator between the gas tank and the machine behind the pressure regulating station.
A stop valve must be installed at the end of the piping between the gas tank and the machine, i.e. at the machine entrance.

4.4 Supply lines for cutting gas supply

Cutting gas connection

To provide the customer with a flexible connection option, special hoses and connectors delivered with the machine are provided by JFY. The customer-provided connection point may thus be found at a radius of 10 meters around the connection point on the machine.

The pipe system must meet the following requirements:

Oil and grease-free lines made of seamless copper piping (refrigerant- and air conditioning-use pipe quality) are required.
The tubes must be processed free of oil and grease.
Fittings must be connected using special connectors (or brass bite-type connector of the same design). Pressure valve connection system is not allowed to use.
All sealing points must be secured with flat gaskets.
Sealants such as liquid Teflon, sealing sprays, lubricants and lubricating pastes, hemp or grease must not be used.
Stainless steel tubes are permitted, but not necessary. If stainless steel tubes are used, stainless steel fittings must also be used.
The pipe ends must be sealed securely to avoid dirt in transit and storage.
Hydraulic hoses do not meet the requirements since they are typically not free of particles.

As a general principle, a stop valve must be mounted before each branch of a ring pipeline in order to make it possible to shut down each ring pipeline segment separately.
