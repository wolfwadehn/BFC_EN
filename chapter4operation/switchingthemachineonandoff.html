Switching the machine on and off

Switching the machine on and off

3.1 Unlocking the EMERGENCY STOP pushbutton

EMERGENCY STOP pushbuttons are located on the operating panel of the laser processing machine and on the safety post of the pallet changer.

To release the EMERGENCY STOP push-button, press it in and turn it in clockwise direction.

3.2 Switching on the machine

Conditions

EMERGENCY STOP is released.
Nobody is within the machine’s danger zone.
Make sure that all the slag carts are in the machine.
Compact dust extractor and other components are switched on.

Not fully functioning compact dust extractor and flying sparks and heat development below the machine.

Do not operate the machine without a slag cart.

  1. Turn on the main switch of the switch cabinet.

  2. Turn on the Main Switch of the machine.

  3. Switch on the machine components

    Switch on the process cooler.
    Switch on the laser device.
    Switch on the system power supply.

  1. Log in HMI interface.

  2. Machine preparation

    Rest machine parameter (No alarm)
    Start the process cooler.
    Start the laser device.

  1. Processing preparation

    Follow calibration.
    Focal point check.
    Co-axiality check.
    Picture loading.
    Gas supply check.

  1. Processing

3.3 Switching off the machine

Condition

No more program is being executed.

  1. Machine is stopped for operation.

  2. Switch-off preparation

    Switch off the laser device.
    Switch off the process cooler.
    Shut down the operating system.
    Switch off the gas supply.

  1. Switch off the machine components

    Switch off the process cooler
    Switch off the laser device.
    Switch off the system power supply.

  1. Turn off the Main Switch of the machine.

  2. Turn off the main switch of the switch cabinet.

The following must be checked before switching on the laser device:

The process cooler is switched on.
No alarm message from process cooler.
Turn key switch 1 to “ON” position, turn MAIN SWITCH 2 to “ON” position.
Power LED is permanently lit in green color, indicating the control unit is switched on.
Please note The laser device must be switched on and off correctly. Incorrect operation will cause damage to the laser device. If no alarm is detected from core modules of the laser device, the RESET command of the TruFiber Cut series laser device shown on the user interface and the RESET signal from the front control panel will not be sent to the core modules. However, if RESET signal from the communication port (software) is received, the core modules will be changed to “OFF” state regardless of the operating status. In case that the TruFiber Cut series laser is under local control, RESET command from the port and front control panel will not reset alarms from the core modules of the laser device. Currently, reset of the core modules can only be performed via FD90 browser.

Switch operation of HMI interface：

Press the softkey image:img/image68.png[img/image68,width=55,height=54] to enable the laser beam. Indicator LED is illuminated.
Press the softkey image:img/image69.png[img/image69,width=54,height=54] to disable the laser beam. Indicator LED is off.

Emergency shutdown and faulty shutdown

Press the EMERGENCY STOP button on the front of the laser device.
Laser is switched off. In particular, the laser beam will not be emitted after the EMERGENCY STOP button is pressed. Control unit of the laser remains active. Red indicator LED (fault) is illuminated.

Switch-on after emergency shutdown.

Turn in clockwise direction to release the E-stop button. After release, the disabled function are available for use.

Check monitoring and error messages

Eliminate the cause of the error
Press the reset button.

Switch-off procedure of the laser device:

Turn off the laser beam.
Turn off the MAIN SWITCH of the laser device.
Turn the key switch to “OFF” position and remove the key.
